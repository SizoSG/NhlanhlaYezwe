Welcome to NHLANHLA YEZWE PROJECTS (PTY) LTD

Over a decade of operational experience since 2013

We are a KwaZulu-Natal–based private company established in 2013, specializing in construction and civil engineering. With CIDB grading of 4CE and 1GB, the company is positioned to deliver both civil infrastructure and general building projects. Under the leadership of Siyabonga Comfort Mseleku, the enterprise continues to operate actively, maintaining compliance with regulatory bodies and focusing on sustainable growth and community impact.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver high‑quality civil engineering and general building services with unwavering compliance to CIPC and CIDB regulations. We are dedicated to empowering local communities through employment and skills development, while ensuring every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards of safety.

Vision

To be a trusted and leading construction partner in KwaZulu-Natal and beyond, delivering sustainable infrastructure and building solutions that uplift communities.
